Travelling Mama

American places I've travelled:

One of the first major places I've travelled to is Honolulu, Hawaii!

I was very fortunate - lucky to have the opportunity to travel to Hawaii. I was 11 years old, and Gitwinksihlkw Four Crest Dancers were set to travel to Hawaii.

Last minute, a few people dropped out. A meeting was called to decide who would fill the spots paid for. Names of cultural dancers were put into a hat,

and my name was chosen. I flew to Hawaii on my 12th Birthday!

Canadian Places I've Travelled:

Also with the Gitwinksihlkw Four Crest Dancers, I've travelled to Canada's capital.

Our dance group performed at the Canadian National Museum, and also toured to the neighbouring province.

  • Ottawa, Ontario
  • Hull, Quebec

After graduating from highschool, I became a volunteer with Katimavik.

I was able to travel to a few different provinces to volunteer:

  • Moose Jaw, Saskatchewan
  • Winnipeg, Manitoba
  • Truro, Nova Scotia
  • Moncton, New Brunswhick
